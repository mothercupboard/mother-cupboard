This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights. 1. What Data We Do Not Collect We do not collect your name, address, phone number, contacts, location data, browsing history, or any biometric data. We do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not serve advertisements. 4. How We Use Your Data Your data is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionality: To maintain your food inventory across your devices To generate AI-powered meal suggestions based on your inventory To send expiry reminder notifications (only if you enable them) To authenticate your account and manage your subscription To diagnose and fix technical issues 5. AI-Powered Features Mother Cupboard uses AI for three features. In each case, data is sent only to process your request and is not used to identify you personally — no email address, device identifier, or account information is included. Meal suggestions — When you request meal suggestions, your inventory item names, quantities, and expiry information are sent to Amazon Web Services (AWS), which uses OpenAI's API to generate personalised recipe ideas. Voice input — When you add items using your voice, your audio recording is sent to OpenAI's Whisper service to transcribe your speech to text. The audio is not stored after transcription. Receipt scanning — When you photograph a receipt or shopping list, the image is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o service to extract food items. Please note that receipt images may contain personal information such as your name or loyalty card number. The image is not stored after processing. OpenAI's data usage policy states that API data is not used to train their models. You can review OpenAI's privacy policy at openai.com/privacy. 6. Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services to operate the App: Supabase (authentication and data sync) — EU-hosted infrastructure Amazon Web Services (backend API) — EU-West-2 (London) region OpenAI (AI features: voice input, receipt scanning, and meal suggestions) — see section 5 Apple App Store (subscription payment processing) Sentry (error monitoring and crash reporting) We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. 7. Data Storage and Security Your inventory data is stored locally on your device and synchronised to our cloud servers hosted in the EU. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or destruction. 8. Data Retention We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Settings → Delete my account), we will permanently remove all your personal data from our servers within 30 days, as required by UK GDPR. Anonymised, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytical purposes. 9.
