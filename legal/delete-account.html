Delete Your Account and Data

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your Mother Cupboard account and the personal data associated with it. Mother Cupboard ("the App") is operated by Mother Cupboard Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales (company number 17256868).

Option 1: Delete your account from within the App

The quickest way to delete your account is directly in the App:

  1. Open Mother Cupboard and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete my account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted as described below.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the App, you can request account and data deletion by emailing us from the address associated with your account:

info@mothercupboard.app

Please include the email address used for your Mother Cupboard account so we can verify your identity. We will action verified requests and respond within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the following personal data from our servers within 30 days:

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • Your inventory data (item names, quantities, expiry dates, storage locations)
  • Your shopping list data
  • Your meal suggestion preferences
  • Your subscription and payment status records held by us
  • Device identifiers and crash logs associated with your account

What data may be retained

Anonymised, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you may be retained for analytical purposes. We may also retain limited records where required to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations. Records of purchases held by Apple or Google are governed by their respective policies and are not controlled by us.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play. To cancel a subscription, manage it in your Apple or Google account settings before deleting your account.
